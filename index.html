My Favorite Movie

All Too Well

As a hardcore Swiftie, my favorite short film is the "All Too Well" music video. Especially at this time of year, I love to watch this short film. It occurs in autumn, and the film features Sadie Sink and Dylan O'Brien.

A Failed Relationship

The film follows the relationship of Taylor Swift and Jake Gyllenhaal. There was a nine year age gap between the two. Taylor Swift was 20 and Jake Gyllenhaal was 29. It seems the relationship ended on her 21st birthday. He didn't show up on her birthday and she ended up crying in a bathroom.

A Happy Ending for Taylor

However, this wasn't the first crack in the relationship. The short film portrays the couple arguing when Jake made fun of Taylor in front of his friends. He refused to help her with housework, and often treated her like an inferior. The two break up, and the film ends by portraying Taylor as a successful author.
